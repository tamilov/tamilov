Create a premium interactive brand landing page for tamilov.com.

Project context:
Tamilov is a personal brand and umbrella ecosystem.
PineApple is a sub-brand / internal engine: a fully automated factory for digital products.
This website must feel like the headquarters of the brand, not a basic portfolio, not a generic agency site, and not a simple business card page.

Primary goal:
Build a visually strong, interactive, modern homepage that introduces the Tamilov brand, presents PineApple as the production engine, and makes the whole ecosystem feel ambitious, productized, and memorable.

VERY IMPORTANT STYLE DIRECTION:
This must be strongly inspired by Gumroad’s design language first, and Lemon Squeezy second.

What is VERY IMPORTANT to capture from Gumroad:
- large, bold, confident typography
- very strong hero section
- clean light background
- thick borders / graphic separation between sections
- playful but tasteful product feel
- minimal but expressive layout
- friendly, direct messaging
- visually iconic composition, not overdesigned
- blocks should feel simple, punchy, memorable
- modern startup / creator economy energy
- slightly experimental, slightly rebellious, but still polished

What to borrow from Lemon Squeezy:
- more seriousness in structure
- cleaner SaaS-like information architecture
- strong product credibility
- structured feature sections
- trustworthy and premium feeling
- polished dashboard/product-system presentation

Critical warning:
Do NOT make this look like a generic dark SaaS template.
Do NOT make it look like a boring agency website.
Do NOT make it overly corporate.
Do NOT make it too futuristic, too neon, too 3D-heavy, or too “AI buzzword” driven.
Do NOT copy Gumroad literally — capture the spirit, hierarchy, boldness, and energy, but create an original design for Tamilov.

Design mood:
“Gumroad energy with Lemon Squeezy discipline”
or
“Playful shell, serious engine”

Visual direction:
- Light theme only
- Large typography with excellent spacing
- Strong section rhythm
- Rounded cards
- Crisp borders
- Elegant shadows, but not too soft
- Clean modern grid
- Editorial + product hybrid feel
- Slightly playful accent color inspired by pineapple / citrus, but subtle and premium
- Base colors should stay refined: off-white, warm white, soft gray, near-black, muted citrus accent
- The pineapple reference should feel intelligent and brand-like, not cartoonish

Website structure:
1. Hero section
- Big headline introducing Tamilov as a brand building digital products, systems, and experiments
- Subheadline explaining that PineApple is the factory / engine behind the ecosystem
- Two strong CTAs:
  - Explore ecosystem
  - Enter PineApple
- Hero should include some interactive visual:
  - product system diagram
  - animated factory nodes
  - ecosystem map
  - dashboard-like motion panel
  - live counters or statuses

2. Brand introduction section
Explain clearly:
- Tamilov is the brand
- PineApple is the engine / factory
- focus areas: digital products, interfaces, automation, experiments, systems
This section should feel premium and concise.

3. “Inside the factory” section
A visually strong section showing the PineApple workflow:
Idea → Validation → Design → Build → Automate → Launch → Improve
Make it interactive with hover states, cards, connectors, or animated nodes.

4. Product ecosystem section
Show product cards with statuses:
- Live
- Beta
- Concept
- Internal
- Archived
Each card should feel like part of a real ecosystem.
PineApple should be featured as the flagship project.

5. Build philosophy section
Show principles such as:
- Systems over chaos
- Launch before perfection
- Design with taste
- Automation with intention
- Build small, scale smart
This section should feel brand-defining and memorable.

6. Notes / journal / experiments section
A lightweight “build in public” style area:
- recent thoughts
- experiments
- prototypes
- ideas in motion
Make this feel alive and current.

7. Work with Tamilov section
Present collaboration opportunities in a productized way, not like a cheap freelance services list.
Examples:
- product landing pages
- branded interfaces
- digital systems
- AI-enhanced workflows
- concept-to-MVP builds

8. Footer / command center
Make footer feel like a mini brand operating system:
- projects
- notes
- contact
- social links
- current status
- ecosystem navigation

Interactivity:
Please include subtle premium interactions:
- hover transitions
- animated counters
- smooth card movement
- command-palette-like navigation option if possible
- section transitions
- animated ecosystem/factory diagram
Keep interactions elegant and fast, not gimmicky.

Technical requirements:
- Build with modern frontend best practices
- Responsive across desktop, tablet, mobile
- Clean code structure
- Reusable components
- Good spacing and typography hierarchy
- Smooth animations
- Performance-conscious
- Use realistic placeholder content for now, but make it feel like a real brand launch

Copy tone:
- confident
- modern
- concise
- premium
- creator-founder energy
- not cringe
- not overhyped
- not corporate jargon heavy

Possible headline directions:
- Tamilov builds digital products, systems, and experiments
- Tamilov is the brand. PineApple is the factory.
- Designed with taste. Built like a system.
- Digital products, interfaces, and infrastructure

Final quality bar:
The result should feel like a brand headquarters for a future digital ecosystem.
It should look distinctive, memorable, modern, and original.
Again: prioritize Gumroad-style boldness, typography, section rhythm, and approachable premium feel above everything else.
